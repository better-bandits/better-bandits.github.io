About

Strange name? Not really. "Bandits" refers to multiarmed bandit algorithms, and "better" to more reliable and more robust algorithms. But we like the Robin Hood connotations too.

Better Bandits was founded by Maarten Speekenbrink, Associate Professor of Mathematical Psychology at University College London (UCL). By developing mathematical models of human learning and decision making, his research forms the foundation of more human-like artificial intelligence. A widely-published and -cited scientist, Maarten brings a broad range of skills and expertise to the table.
